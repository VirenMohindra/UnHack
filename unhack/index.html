Cyber Knight
for classes 6th to 8th

Children are digital natives- they are ‘always online’ connecting with people and sharing their personal information. The program exposes them to the ‘cyber world’ and takes them on a virtual tour of how their information travels in the cyberspace, what are the concerns and the repercussions- why is it important to protect their privacy on social networking sites like Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, etc.

Digitally Unplugged
for classes 9th to 12th

Today, the Internet has enabled instant global communication and access to information- students communicate, collaborate, create and accomplish results never seen before – intellectual energy thus released is transforming education globally. Unfortunately, other problems like Cyber bullying amongst teens is on the rise. Digitally Unplugged empowers children to harness this potential and ‘stay safe online’.

Digital Leader
for teachers

Technology is transforming education. The program exposes teachers to emerging disruptive technologies in education helps them design and develop digital-age learning experiences and assessments. Teachers learn to use social media as a tool for collaborative learning and building professional communities. The Program for Educators is an interactive workshop that empowers teachers to be digital leaders.

Parents NEXT
for parents

Today we live in a hyper-connected world- from smartphones to smartwatches to wearables. At school, they have access to the Internet to facilitate work and enhance their learning experience. Often it’s challenging and even frightening for parents to navigate the digital world their children inhabit. Parents NEXT is a cyber awareness initiative that focuses on enabling and empowering parents in the digital age.
