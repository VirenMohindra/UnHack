A little bit about Digitally Unplugged

Executive Summary

Children are wired to learn digitally and come with handhelds practically attached to their limbs. They wake up abruptly to the continuous ringing sound of notifications, they grab their smartphones – they just need their daily morning dose of Snapchat, Instagram and Facebook. At school, they have access to digital technologies, and the Internet to facilitate work and enhance their learning experience. The Internet has enabled instant global communication and access to information, where students use information at their fingertips, collaborate and create to accomplish results never seen before – intellectual energy thus released is transforming education globally. Social media has given immense power to children to easily spread their ideas to an audience of thousands. Hence it becomes imperative to educate them to become responsible digital citizens as well as discerning users of everything the internet has to offer in this globally collaborative world. UnHack program focuses on teaching students how to learn and innovate by using technology to their advantage. The Program is an interactive workshop to empower students to harness the opportunities provided by digital learning; to inculcate digital ethics and to stay safe online.


Workshop Session Details:
DIGITAL LITERACY - Enhances performance and inspires innovations and entrepreneurial ventures

  • Search Literacy
  • Social Media Literacy
  • Collaboration, Creativity and Innovation

DIGITAL ETHICS - Inculcates morality in online behavior
  • Appropriate handling of Content
  • Appropriate use of Identity
  • Social Media Ethics

CYBER SAFETY AND SECURITY - Security ensures that children are safe online protected from cyber bullies, sexual predators, cybercriminals and inappropriate content
  • Cyberbullying
  • Inappropriate Content and Sexual Solicitation
  • Protecting Data, Identity and Privacy
  • Device Safety
  • Cyber Laws
